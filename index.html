About TRBA

The Road Burners Association (TRBA) is a unified platform bringing together motorcycle clubs and enthusiasts. We promote safe riding, organize events, and foster a strong community of passionate riders.

  • Representing 20+ motorcycle clubs
  • Monthly group rides and events
  • Safety training programs
  • Community outreach initiatives

Our Mission

To create a safe, inclusive, and vibrant motorcycle community in Bengaluru by:

  • Promoting responsible riding
  • Supporting local motorcycle clubs
  • Organizing community events
  • Advocating for rider rights
